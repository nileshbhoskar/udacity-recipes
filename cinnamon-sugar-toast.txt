Ingredients:
 - 1 slice bread (choose your favorite)
 - 2 teaspoons* butter
 - 1 teaspoon* sugar
 - 1 teaspoon* cinnamon
 * to taste and size of bread

Directions:
 1. Mix cinnamon and sugar into an easy to use sprinkling container.

 2. Toast bread as desired.

 3. Butter evenly while still hot.

 4. Evenly coat buttered side with cinnamon-sugar mixture to taste.
